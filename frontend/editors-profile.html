Editor's profile

Venugopal hails from Chintalapudi of West godavari district of Andhra Pradesh. Born in a Vysya business family to Sri Satyanarayana and Smt. Naga mani as 2nd son in his maternal grand parent's village namely Adamilli in West godavari district in year 1974. Completed his graduation in Commerce and joined his father's jewellery business in Chintalapudi. As part of business expansion, his father proposed to extend the same business to then rapidly growing near by town Janga Reddy Gudem, which is being look after by him to date.

He is married to Smt. Varalakshmi from Aswaraopet, Khammam district and they are blessed with two daughters Chy. Mounica and Chy. Satvika who are studying IPCC in Guntur and 9th standard in Janga Reddy Gudem.

A leader is a leader always. With the gifted leadership qualities in Shri Venugopal, he is serving in various business/service organizations within country as well as internation organizations. He is serving as the president of The Jangareddy Gudem bullion merchant's association aprat from communal organizations in local. He served as Governor of Vasavi clubs international District V-202A consisting of West and East godavari districts of Andhra pradesh during the year 2011 and from them onwards serving the needy through the Vasavi Clubs International as International Director in various departments such as organizing free book distribution to needy poor students from Intermediate and above level.

In addition to this, he used to encourage economically weaker students for education by paying fees to huge number of students regularly. Keeping the experience in view and observations made on the youth, he thought of becoming a trainer and motivator for which he has spent huge amount of time, talent and treasure. He has felt that if the youth is trained well in the country, our country will be excellent in the world. To discharge resposibility as citizen of this great country India, he has opted for this profession. At last he has graduated as a trainer and motivator and became an official trainer in the year 2012. From then onwards he has given trainings in various colleges like Mater Minds, Aditya group, Rise group, Ongole, Suryapet, Markapuram, Palakol, Koyyalagudem, Dubacherla, Thadipatri, Mariped banglaw, Vijayawada, Bhimavaram, Jangareddygudem and various other places where the government and private colleges have utilized the services of Shri Venugopal.

He is proud to claim that he has conducted and seminars and trained, motivated 63000+ students till now.

Apart from student trainings he has given excellent counselling to the couple who wants to part in their life and made them to be happy couple which gave him excellent satisfaction in his life.

His wish is to make the Indian youth as world's champions.
